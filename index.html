What we teach

Judo

Judo is a Japanese martial art centred on throws and grappling. It emphasises using an opponent's strength against them. Judo has been an olympic sport since 1964 and was popularised in the UFC with Ronda Rousey.

Karate

Karate is a Japanese martial art focusing on strikes, including punches and kicks. It involves precise and controlled movements, with an emphasis on kata and self-discipline.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), is a ground-focused martial art using leverage, joint locks, and chokeholds for control and submissions. It's known for adaptability and effectiveness in ground combat, it is one of the main martial arts used in Mixed Martial Arts training.

Why us

Fusion Fighters Martial Arts started off as a small outfit, running individual classes out of school gyms, pub function rooms and church halls. All the way up to our world class training facility that can accommodate over 200 students.

This hard work took 10 long years to finally achieve, so believe us when we say that we know a thing or two about perseverance and dedication. Here at Fusion Fighters, we strive to bring the best out of anyone who steps onto the mat, instilling that dedication and perseverance in each and every one of our students to make you the best you can be.

We offer a unique blend of martial arts to help you become a well rounded fighter. We focus on three main aspects of fighting that we think will achieve any goal you may have as a martial artist.

We use Judo as our, what we like to call, physical strength martial art. This is because Judo is basically throwing people around. This builds extremely good core strength and agility.

We use Karate as our striking based martial art, most of our instructors have a background in Karate and this is because of the focus it gives you along with the arsenal of punches, kicks and sweeps.

We used Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu as our grappling or ground-work martial art, this is because it is so effective at subduing opponents larger than you and is great for self defence.
